Plant Monitoring System

In the era of smart homes and automation, nurturing your plants has become a tech-savvy endeavor. In this project, I explore a captivating maker project â€” a Plant Monitoring System that not only let me keep a vigilant eye on my plants' well-being but also incorporates a DHT22 temperature and humidity sensor. This intelligent system, built around an ESP32 S2 mini, capacitive soil moisture sensors, and the DHT22 sensor, seamlessly integrates with Home Assistant for an enhanced plant care experience.

Components

Main Board - ESP32 S2 Mini:

  • The central unit, responsible for powering and collecting measurements.
  • Programmed with ESPHome in Home Assistant for seamless integration and customization.

Soil Moisture Sensors:

  • Four widely available capacitive sensors offer precise soil moisture data.
  • Essential for monitoring the hydration levels of your plants.

DHT22 Temperature and Humidity Sensor:

  • Provides additional environmental data, enhancing your understanding of the plant's surroundings.
  • Offers insights into temperature and humidity conditions crucial for plant health.

3D-Printed Cases:

  • The 3D-printed cases not only protect the components but also add a touch of aesthetics to your plant monitoring system.
  • 3D models were designed by Bruno and are available on Printables: Sensor Case Main Board

Project Integration

The seamless integration of this Plant Monitoring System with Home Assistant opens up a world of possibilities for plant care automation. The collected data, including soil moisture, temperature, and humidity, is uploaded to the Home Assistant server via WiFi, allowing you to monitor and manage your plants remotely.

Key Features

  • Remote Monitoring:
    • Access real-time soil moisture, temperature, and humidity data from anywhere using Home Assistant.
    • Stay connected to your plants even when you're away.
  • Automation:
    • Leverage Home Assistant's automation capabilities to create custom routines based on multiple environmental factors.
    • Set up watering schedules or receive notifications when your plants need attention.
  • User-Friendly Interface:
    • The intuitive Home Assistant interface makes it easy to visualize and interpret the collected data.
    • Effortlessly manage multiple plants with a user-friendly dashboard.
